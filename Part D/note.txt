1. Network
Selected Nodes: Germany (DE) as the hub, connected to France (FR), Switzerland (CH), and Austria (AT).
Capacity: based on the maximum annual physical flows (2017) from ENTSO-E to represent existing grid limits.
2. Simulation
Co-optimization: The whole system is jointly optimized for the lowest total cost.
Technology Mix:
FR: Fixed existing Nuclear capacity (baseload).
CH & AT: Included Hydro to reflect Alpine resource reality.
DE & Others: Extendable Wind, Solar, and OCGT.
Generally speaking, different countries rely on different generation technologies. Here I choose the
main 4 technologies for each country (3 for CH).
